Referral partner

Recommend ChatApp service to clients, partners and earn on each selling.

How it works

You are an official partner of CRM or ERP, attracting new clients via referral links

To attract clients and partners use personal recommendations or Internet promotion, send referral link to ChatApp connection via messengers, e-mail, post it on your website, in social media, use messaging and other tools. It’s important to attract at least 1 new client in 3 months.

ChatApp sells and supports the product

After receipt of applications from your clients, ChatApp employees contact with them and hold consultations, sell the product, and render technical support.

You get a reward

You get a reward in the amount of 10% of all payments made by clients attracted by you, as long as they pay. If no clients have joined ChatApp for 3 months in a row based on your recommendation, payments are suspended until a new client joins. Renumeration is paid on a monthly basis

Requirements to a partner

Allowable for CRM integrators. You need to be an official partner of CRM or ERP systems

All you need is to recommend ChatApp to your clients and send a referral link

You need to attract at least 1 new client in 3 months
